Journal Entry
08.21.00
So today was the day - I went off to school to officially put together my Fall Senior Year schedule.

I thought it was going to be a problem, seeing as how I am in line to pick in the second half of the school students. I was aiming for a number of classes, including AP C++, AP Economics, and Weight Training. Since aiming also means with the right teacher, I thought I was screwed for sure, but I was proven wrong.

After solving a quick problem with my scheduling sheet (green sheet), I went off to sign up for the classes in the gym. One after the other, I went through my check off list and from desk to desk signing up for all I wanted with the most ease ever.

I was done and out in 15 minutes and on my way to work. I really thought that I was going to have a tougher time than this. I am really happy things ended the way they did because from previous semesters it's not as easy as that.
.That was the scheduling, but now it's the tough part that comes next. Tomorrow is the first official day of school. That means back to classes, lectures, and homework. I hope that I get use to it fast and not like last year when it took a whole semester to adjust.

I think it will all be good though. By the way, I want to apologize for repeating myself in entries to those who read all of them. I do it for the passing reader.
June 2000
