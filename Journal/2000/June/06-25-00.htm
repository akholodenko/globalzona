Journal Entry
06.25-26-27.00
Wow, I can't believe how fast time really flies. Seems like I just wrote a journal entry, when in reality it has been three days. Now lets see what has been going on during those days.

The 25th I spend wasting at home waiting for a friend to call and let me know when we would go to get a present for a mutual friend of ours for that guy's birthday. If you read the previous entry, you would know that we were suppose to go yesterday, but that didn't happen. Finally around 4pm I was picked up and after getting a third friend, the three of us went to the local shopping center, Stonestown Galleria, to find a good present.

We searched for a while and couldn't find anything. I originally suggested a watch from Macy's. But that idea was struck down. Not cool enough. So we walked for a couple of hours until finally ended up at Macy's buying a watch. Who da man?
On the 26th my day was mostly similar during the day time. I went to school. That followed itself up with five hours of work, and then a long trip home.

However, in the evening, my friends and I went out for pizza, to celebrate friend's b-day and give him his watch present, along with just plain hanging out, which the four of us haven't done for a while.

After having some good pizza over some great jokes, we went for a walk around the area and bumped into a playground; one like everyone of us had been to at a young child age. There we started talking about the funnies things - bathroom experiences.

Girls have it easy with their leather couches and make-up rooms, while guys have a door, urinal, and may be a stall that has been violated by aliens. So we came up with the greatest idea. We would reenact the scenes, exaggerating the humorous aspects as skits, such as Saturday Night Live or Mad TV skits and tape them with a digital camera.

Folks, the second those short skits are done and edited you will be easily able to download them from Global Zona.
On the 27th, that being today, I felt lazy. I didn't go to work because after working a while straight last week and then picking up where I left off this time, I need some time off. I am not use to this, since I only worked a couple of days a week during the school year. The atmosphere is just not appealing all the time.

So I went home after school and simply watched TV and surfed online. God, I miss the simple things in life.
June 2000
