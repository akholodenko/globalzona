Journal Entry
06.08.00
Just got back from work now and have some kind of disappointing news. I asked and was declined a raise. But hey, I understand, the guy can't afford it right now. So I thought I would make it up with the amount of hours I would work - wrong. I worked my usual amount. I guess that there is just no hope.

I thought there would be a lot of work to be done since my boss was out for a couple of weeks, but turns out that I was able to handle everything and don't even need to come in tomorrow, cause there's nothing to do. See, at times like this I wish I had an official 9-5 job where no matter what, would always be something to do and I would have to be there.

Oh, some good news on the other hand, my AllAdvantage.com checks came in yesterday. They summed up to $50 and while it doesn't seem like big bucks, it's more than nothing. If you want to earn free money, click here and sign up. You'll have to download their viewbar and run it while online. It takes up about an inch of your screen, but it's like nothing at all.
Hey, if you like GlobaZona.com and want to get our mail updates, go here, and sign up. Then, you'll get a notification in the your inbox, to which you'll have to reply. That's all. We do have a lot of sections in the site, and have you been to all? There are the main news sections: Music, Movies, TV, Celebs, in addition to the Special. Check them out!
Let's talk about music for second. Out of all the musicians that are out there, I look up to and appreciate those that have been around the longest. The reason why, is because those guys have been able to evolve with the time and still appeal to an audience, years if not decades after their original debut. It's whether they changed their style or their style was so universal that no matter what, it was still cool and hip. Like take the Rolling Stones and Aerosmith. They guys have been here forever, and they are packing in concerts more than ever before.

This is of course the exact opposite of the pop culture hits, even besides the one-hit-wonders. N*Sync for example. Yeah they are cool now, but what about five or seven years down the road? Do these guys seem to have a universal or unique appeal? I don't think so. Britney Spears can be added to the bunch as well.

I mean really, how many artists are remembered as being legendary? Very few, compared to the number out there total. Does anyone remember "New Kids on the Block"? I do, but too embarrassed to talk about it. And they were huge only ten years ago! Mark my words, in ten years people will not only forget today's pop hits, but joke about their existence.
