Journal Entry
06.02.00
So now that summer is upon us, and boredom has not set in yet, a journal will be appearing with updates to itself. New comments on life and thoughts will appear on daily or semi - daily bases. This journal will be kept by the webmaster of GlobalZona.com and will deal with the personal adventures of the narrator.

Yesterday I attended the graduation of the San Francisco Lowell High School Class of 2000. Surprisingly quick, the event was a sad end to a four - year run of the education career of the students. Some where sad, others, sad they didn't leave earlier, all in all, a mood of anxiety stood in the air.

Graduation is a heart-felt experience all the time, sometimes more for those who haven't reached yet, than for those who have. Being one to have to stick around for another year of high, I truelly wish it was already open. I know I am going to feel sad when my last year of high school comes to an end, but I want that feeling, I want to feel sad, because I want to move on to college.

One thing I am totally looking forward to and hoping for in my college experience is to be able to share it with my best friend, Artem M. (ThePocket.net), who I have known since Friday, September 17, 1992. We have similar scholastic achievements and will probably be attending the same place of higher learning. I think that it is fun to go to a new place and experience new things, but it is even more fun to share it with an old friend.
June 2000
