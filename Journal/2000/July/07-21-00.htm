Journal Entry
07.21.00
Now it is down to less than 24 hours until my plane take off I will be taken 1/2 way around the world to a society I have never encountered before. My life will be turned up side down - nah, just a vacation.

My girlfriend should be coming over in about an hour to help me pack my remaining stuff and say good bye. I am really going to miss her during this time, especially, since our 16-month anniversary is going to be during the time that I am gone. I set up a nifty eCard to go out on that date, so while I am not here in person, I am still here in spirit.

Yesterday a friend and I went to see the X-Men movie. We had previous plans that fell apart, and then ones that came together in the last minute. It was just the two of us and it was good.

The film was great, but not 100% of what I expected. Unfortunately, there wasn't as large of a variety of mutants as I hoped for the film to contain. A hand of full of good guys fought a smaller hand of bad guys with some minor characters in the picture.

But you have to understand the film creators. The X-Men story is too vast to tell in one movie, that's why this one was all right. A type of introduction, which will hopefully be followed by a number of sequels is completely welcome in this realm.
I was glad to go and do something with that friend of mine, because we hadn't really done anything for some time and it was getting odd. Also, since I'm going to be gone for over two weeks, I wanted to see people before I left.

I think that my Israel experience will be something to remember and cherish, but I hope that I don't miss out on anything back here in San Francisco. Hey, even if I do, I have my people who will fill me in on the details of the time.

So I am just sitting and typing here and waiting and waiting. I can't even piece together my idea of how the trip is going to be. I know one thing, I'm not going to be cold at any point of the time.

I am also hoping to attend one of the famous Israel dance clubs/discos, which are supposedly very good. I don't know if I will be able to, but I hope so. I also heard that the silver and other stuff like that is cheaper in Israel, so I am also going to be looking into that action.

I guess that this is farewell for close to three weeks. I will be writing a journal while traveling, and I might put it up on the site- we'll see how personal it is. Otherwise, check out the material that is presently here, because there isn't going to be anything changed for a while.
