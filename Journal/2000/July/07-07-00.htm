Journal Entry
07.07.00
Whoever said that a person only notices and misses things when they are gone was completely on the money. It was no joke. It is no joke. I have experienced that wonderful piece of life yesterday, with a swift kick in the balls (emotionally, not physically).

I can't tell you what happened, but all I can say is that I did give smoking and drinking a though. However, I was convinced by an outside force not to do so, due to the fact that I am a stronger person now and I can handle the problem without such items.

But I hate getting slapped in the face with the truth, like any other human being would. Especially when the truth is one of your most feared and hatted nightmares coming true and all you can do is hit your head against the wall and ask yourself about how you can tell that happen. I, in general, love life, but depending on the results or events of the upcoming two weeks I will whether kill all that there is emotional and caring in life body or do everything I can to flourish it. Unfortunately I have a strong feeling that death will come to me and my heart.

I am an extremely depressed individual right now. Those of you who have been there and are understanding the reasons from my words - you how it feels. I sometimes hope for so minor tragedy to happen to me, nothing that would cause permanent damage, but something where I would be in the hospital for a couple of days. Because then, in a time of crisis, I would really test the care of the people around me. Sometimes I really don't know who cares about me and who doesn't. I am not talking about parents or relatives or anything like that. I am talking about those people who are on a different level with me and who supposedly love me - or at least at one point did. I am a person who is serious about love and will not have people telling me things like that while in the back of their heads they are thinking the exact opposite because that is just wrong in the world to do.

Playing with my feelings is not what I am talking about. I am talking about the strength of love. The power of it. If two people love each other, most actions (excluding cheating or something like that) are not basis for the end of all. It is not and when someone is mad, they should calm down and think about things rationally before making life-altering decisions.
Life is short. People shouldn't spend it arguing and getting upset about little things. Some small things are hurting, but everything should be taken lightly, and then if it still seems deep, then do something about it. But the main thing is to chill.
June 2000
