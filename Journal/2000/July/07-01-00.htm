Journal Entry
07.1.00
A new month marks a new beginning. Not. Everything is still the same. School is school. Work is work. Except, now that I am working more, my pay checks have been getting pleasantly incremented. I like that.

We are approaching the all-mighty holiday, "Forth of July" USA independence celebration! Yeah! What does this really? I think I know - sales in stores. But are they really sales. No. All the stores do, is mark up the prices 20% and then simply give the customer a whopping 15% discount. Now that's a bargain.

Oh, yeah. Another thing. This year, the 4-th is on a Tuesday. Since it is a national holiday, it does not get moved closer to the weekend. Fine. However, many corporations and schools are requiring the students to attend school of the Monday, July 3rd. That is absurd. Yet we still get our holiday, with a chopped up week of Tuesday off. How weird is that?

It's ridiculous. In the summer semester they also have the stupid rule of three absences and you're out/ So if you are hanging on by a string anyway, there goes a perfectly swell four-day weekend. I mean, why not make the holiday a pleasant one with four days off. It's not like we're learning anyway. It's all complete crap if you ask me. All we do in class is sit, read tedious chapters out loud and then answer simple questions and write down vocabulary, for no reason, since we don't get tested, just to see that we write it.

Well I can write, I don't have to prove it to myself every day you know. It is not as difficult of a thing as one might think, for someone who is going to be a high school senior in the 43rd best school in the nation. Come on!

That's okay, our teacher sweetens the pot with his 15-year-old stories about theft and him being to proud to run for the bus. "Well tough cookie", as he says himself. We are not being paid $200 at hour to listen to his problems. He is getting paid for talking about them. But that's fine too, he doesn't get paid that much.
On to brighter information. Tonight my girlfriend and I are off to this cool restaurant called The Stinking Rose. All the food that they serve is equipped with garlic. The place even got a visit from the cast of The Real World: San Francisco during the taping of that show.

The place is located in the popular North Beach district of San Francisco. The place is full of dinning, dancing, and even stripping.
July 2000
