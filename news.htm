Weekend Movie: Y Tu Mama Tambien
::05::07::02::
Directed by Mexican-born, New York-based Alfonso Cuaron, this movie broke box-office records and stirred controversy when it opened in Mexico this summer. With their girlfriends away on a European holiday, 17-year-old friends Julio and Tenoch anticipate a summer of promiscuity. Soon, though, they meet Luisa, the 28-year-old wife of Tenoch’s cousin who changes their sexual lives forever. Read the full review...
