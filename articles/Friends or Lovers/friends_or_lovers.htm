FRIENDS OR LOVERS

        Friends or Lovers? Isn't that an interesting question. Is it physically and realistically possible to guy and girls to be friends with no sexual innuendo involved from both sides. Experts say no. Dr. Drew Pinsky and Adam Corolla or the popular television / radio show "Loveline" giving that simply answer.
        Why is it so unheard of guys and girls being "just friends". Well it's simple, usually, one of the two people in the friendship have more than that on their mind. The entire process of such "friendships" is a common trend of out of nowhere getting to know each other very well and being good friends. Then the asking to hang out comes into play. The one interested is usually doing the asking, while the other person is doing the rejecting. It gets to the point of the one desiring the deeper relationship getting tired of beating around the bush and asking out the other person right out. They get an explanation on how they should be friends because they are thought of as a brotherly/sisterly figure, and nothing more. This of course is the mark where the friendship flourishes, yet it never (almost) does. Usually the people stop talking as often, and then they just become "hi" friends. [People who simply say hi to each other when they run into each other, and nothing else].
        One of the problems with girls and friendships with guy is that girls treat the male friends to a point as their boyfriends. This is done by asking for things to be done for them out of the blue and getting pissed of if the guy is unable or unwilling to do what is asked. However, they do not think about returning the favor ever. The requests keep building, while no return is shown. I understand that guys are suppose to be all nice to girls and everything, but this isn't even the '90s anymore - it's further down the road to sexual independence. Here everyone is alike, and if you want a favor in the future, do one for that person now.
        When you do one favor, it doesn't end there. Since you have shown yourself as a weak target for favoritism, you are constantly bombarded with request for various help, without a second thought of doing by themselves. So you put out a personality of being nice and when you turn a girl down on help once, right away you are a bad guy and have changed.
        If a guy has a dilemma, you don't see him going to a girl and asking for help. He will do no matter what to squeeze the ability to complete the task on his own [I am that way, regarding asking anyone for any help, I feel I am more dependent]. I don't understand the thing with girls, are they just dependent on always being helped? I am asking for input from GlobalZona.com female readers.
