MILLENNIUM TEEN MATING

        With the passing of time comes a new era in many things, including the ritual of dating. Yet, as much as things change is just as much as they stay the same. Some of the most primitive items girls look for in a guy are still cars, looks, and cool-rating. While the factors that determine each have evolved, the basic principles are still at work.
        Some of the popular teen gatherings are large, public parties. Some of these include night activities run by local teen organizations and clubs. Such events draw the attention of many, since everyone of their friends can come without crashing the party. Usually the turn out is high, while the productivity level is low. Everyone tries to impress each other of course. These days in the new millennium we have cell phones, parents' expensive rides, and clothing to fill up that category. If you think about it, that is all a piece of crap, but hey,. If you are looking for a person using those methods, you will get exactly who you're looking for.
        When all of the games pieces are in place, the players begin their moves. Going from person to person, they rekindle old memories of how things were with people they barely know and have seen once in the last couple of years. However, to the untrained eye, it seems as though the person is extremely popular and very well knows all of the people. This especially impresses those prepubescent girls who don't have the experience to know any better.
        In the end what happens is always the same. Those who come with their girlfriends end up having a good time, because they have someone to dance with, while those who come alone chill with their friends, migrating from one side of the dance floor to another, and form the inside to the outside.
        In addition, what usually takes place in the world of teen lovin', is that the older guys get the younger girls, because it is easier for them to impress the innocent females. However, for some guys, including myself, it is difficult to go out with someone who is too young for me, because it seems like I should be dating someone my own age, while that person is already dating someone older. It is a tied loop and keeps on going for ages; yet what I observed was that while the girls compound with new additions every year, the guys stay about the same, just get older. Sometimes you see a guy always dating girls the same age, no matter how old he is [15, 16, 17,. 18…+]. It makes no difference, still he is dating girls who are like 15 or 16. What to do, what to do.
