The Big Day
By
Artem Kholodenko
No matter how different or similar, there are certain moments when differences are put to the side and everyone becomes an equal. In American, that time is the annual football Super Bowl, in which two of the toughest teams battle it out for the ultimate prize. Millions tune in to watch the 4-hour event, while being taken advantage of by the commercialists who cough up big bucks for 30 seconds of airtime.
The game however, is just part of the experience, with the half-time show and other attractions like the cheerleaders being the other aspects. Sometimes it's a wonder that people could actually stop fighting and come together as one - the fan powerhouse. One the other hand there is a new hope. The reason for that is if people could be brought together like this once, it can be done more, and may be some day the world will become a utopia of heavenly peace and prosperity. May be one day people will be concerned more about others than themselves.
Although the "american dream" is a great thing, many people step on others to reach wealth. There only one important thing that everyone should remember: those who you step - on, on the way up might be the same people you need the most on the way down. So remember, that guy in middle management who you boss around might be bossing you around if you don't play your cards right. Life is funny that way, so laugh with it and don't let it laugh at you.
