Shaft

Starring: Samuel L. Jackson, Jeffery Wright and Christian Bale

Directed by: John Singleton

Rating: R    Parents' Rating Guide

Running Time: 98 min.

SYNOPSIS: John Singleton updates the classic 70s Blaxploitation movie and casts Samuel Jackson as a modern day Shaft. In this story, Shaft is a police detective trying to catch a murderer before he kills the only witness of his last crime, played by Toni Collette.
