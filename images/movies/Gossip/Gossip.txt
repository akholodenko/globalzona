Gossip

Starring James Marsden, Norman Reedus and Joshua Jackson
Directed by Davis Guggenheim
Rated R

Synopsis 
--------
A group of college students plant a rumor and create a web of gossip 
which spins so out of control that one man is falsely accused of a 
crime and a woman kills herself. Marsden stars as a pre-law student 
and Reedus as his admiring friend.
