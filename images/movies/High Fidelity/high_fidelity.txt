High Fidelity

Starring John Cusack, Iben Hjejle, Lisa Bonet and Tim Robbins
Directed by Stephen Frears
Rated R
Running time: 107 min.

Synopsis
--------
Adapted from the popular Nick Hornby novel, this is the story of a 
thirty-something record store owner (Cusack) who is forced to confront 
his arrested adolescence when his girlfriend (Hjejle) dumps him.
