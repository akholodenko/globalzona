Hamlet

Starring Ethan Hawke, Kyle Maclachlan, Sam Shepard and Julia Stiles
Directed by Michael Almereyda
Rated R
Running time: 111 min.

Synopsis 
--------
In this modern update of the classic play, Ethan Hawke stars as the heir to the Denmark Corporation. Rounding out the cast in this neo-Shakespearian New York ensemble are Kyle MacLachlan, Bill Murray, Liev Schreiber, and Julia Stiles. The movie premiered at Sundance
