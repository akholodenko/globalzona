Boys & Girls

Starring: Freddie Prinze Jr., Clare Forlani and Jason Biggs

Directed by: Robert Iscove

Rating: PG-13    Parents' Rating Guide

Running Time: 98 min.

SYNOPSIS: Freddie Prinze Jr. plays opposite Claire Forlani in this romantic comedy about two longtime friends who go to college at Berkeley. After they both suffer a string of failed romances, they start to think maybe they should be dating.
