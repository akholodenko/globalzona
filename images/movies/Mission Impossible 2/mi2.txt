Mission: Impossible 2  

Starring: Tom Cruise, Ving Rhames, Thandie Newton and Dougray Scott

Directed by: John Woo

Rating: PG-13    Parents' Rating Guide

Running Time: 126 min.

SYNOPSIS: Cruise reprises the role of Ethan Hunt in this sequel to the 1996 action hit, directed this time around by Hong Kong maestro John Woo. Hunt and his associates pit themselves against the threat of biological warfare and a former agent-turned-villain (Scott) who schemes against them. His ex-love (Newton) -- who is Hunt's current bedmate -- is sent undercover to try to foil the enemy.
