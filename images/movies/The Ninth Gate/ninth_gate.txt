The Ninth Gate

Starring Johnny Depp, Frank Langella and Lena Olin
Directed by Roman Polanski
Rated R
Running time: 127 min.

Synopsis
--------
Dean Corso (Depp) is a rare book tracker hired by European book 
collector and professor of demonology Boris Balkan (Langella) to find 
the remaining copies of a 17th-century series entitled The Nine Gates 
of the Shadow Kingdom. As his quest takes him from New York to Paris 
to Madrid, Corso begins to suspect the motives behind his assignment 
when he learns the books are said to be capable of summoning Satan.
