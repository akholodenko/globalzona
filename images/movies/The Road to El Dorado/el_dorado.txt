The Road to El Dorado

Starring Armand Assante and Kenneth Branagh
Directed by Bibo Bergeron and Will Finn
Rated PG
Running time: 83 min.

Synopsis
--------
Kevin Klein, Kenneth Branagh, and Armand Assante are the voices behind 
this feature-length animated movie set in 16th century Mayan culture. 
Two Spanish stable hands stow away on Cortez's ship and accidentally 
discover El Dorado, the Lost City of Gold.
