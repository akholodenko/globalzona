American Psycho

Starring Christian Bale, Willem Dafoe, Jared Leto and Chloe Sevigny
Directed by Mary Harron
Rated R
Running time: 97 min.

Synopsis 
--------
Sandra Bullock plays a journalist who is forced to spend twenty-eight days in rehab after 
she's arrested for drunk driving. While in the rehab center, she meets and befriends two 
other patients trying to overcome their addictions, played by Viggo Mortensen and Marianne
