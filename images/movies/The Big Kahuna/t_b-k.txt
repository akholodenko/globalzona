The Big Kahuna

Starring Kevin Spacey, Danny DeVito and Peter Facinelli
Directed by John Swanbeck
Rated R
Running time: 90 min.

Synopsis
--------
Three lubricant salesmen (Spacey, DeVito, Facinelli) scheme to land the biggest account of their careers. Meeting at a Wichita convention, they plan to host a reception, hoping to turn their luck around. When one is invited to an event with a big client, he becomes involved in a chain of events that affects all of their lives.
