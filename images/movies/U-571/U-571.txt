U-571

Starring Matthew McConaughey, Bill Paxton, Harvey Keitel and David Keith
Directed by Jonathan Mostow
Rated PG-13
Running time: 108 min.

Synopsis 
--------
U-571 is a white-knuckle World War II suspense drama about an American 
submarine crew's battle against time - and their own fears - while 
carrying out a daring mission to capture a top-secret encrypting 
device from a Nazi U-Boat.
