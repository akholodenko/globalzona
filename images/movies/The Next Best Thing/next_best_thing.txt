The Next Best Thing

Starring Madonna and Rupert Everett
Directed by John Schlesinger
Rated PG-13

Synopsis
-------
When Abbie (Madonna) and her gay best friend Robert (Everett) decide 
to raise a baby together, neither is prepared for how the experience 
will affect their relationship. When Abbie falls for another man 
(Benjamin Bratt), Robert won't give up his claim on the child and the 
two end up in a custody battle in this comedy from veteran director 
John Schlesinger.
