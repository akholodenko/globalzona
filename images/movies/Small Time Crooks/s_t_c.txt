Small Time Crooks

Starring Michael Rapaport, Tracy Ullman, Hugh Grant and Woody Allen
Directed by Woody Allen
Rated PG
Running time: 95 min.

Synopsis
--------
An ex-con dishwasher and his manicurist wife pursue their dreams of becoming rich by planning and executing a clever bank robbery. Woody Allen stars in a cast which also includes Jon Lovitz and comedienne Elaine May.
