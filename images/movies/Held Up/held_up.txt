Held Up

Starring Jamie Foxx, Jake Busey, Nia Long and Julie Hagerty
Directed by Steve Rash
Rated PG-13

Synopsis 
--------
Michael Dawson (Foxx) manages to lose his fiancee and his vintage car in a matter of minutes when his road trip through the Grand Canyon goes horribly wrong. The last straw comes when he is taken hostage in a convenience store in the desert. Foxx translates his TV popularity to the big screen.
