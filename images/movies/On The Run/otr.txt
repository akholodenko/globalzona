On The Run  

Starring: Michael Imperioli and John Ventimiglia

Directed by: Bruno De Almeida

Rating: NR    Parents' Rating Guide

Running Time: 94 min.

SYNOPSIS: Michael Imperioli plays Albert, a shy travel agent living a solitary life in New York City. His life is turned upside down when Louie, his best friend from childhood who has just escaped from prison, appears on his doorstep begging for help. What ensues is one long, crazy night that will change both of their lives forever.
