Praise

Starring: Peter Fenton and Sacha Horler

Directed by: John S, Curran

Rating: R    Parents' Rating Guide

Running Time: 98 min.

SYNOPSIS: This Australian import tells the dramatic story of two misfits engaged in a sexually charged relationship. When a nymphomaniac with eczema (Sacha Horler) moves in with a chain-smoking asthmatic (Peter Fenton), both of their lives are turned upside down by their deep erotic connection.
