Pitch Black

Starring Cole Hauser, Vin Diesel and Radha Mitchell
Directed by David Twohy
Rated R
Running time: 107 min

Synopsis
--------
When a damaged ship transporting passengers is forced to crash-land, 
the survivors end up on a seemingly lifeless planet. But when an 
eclipse throws them into complete darkness, the planet suddenly erupts 
with vicious, deadly creatures. Together, they must race from the 
crash site through a steep, treacherous canyon to escape these 
nocturnal aliens and reach the ship alive.
