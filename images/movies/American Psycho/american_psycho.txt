American Psycho

Starring Christian Bale, Willem Dafoe, Jared Leto and Chloe Sevigny
Directed by Mary Harron
Rated R
Running time: 97 min.

Synopsis 
--------
Based on Bret Easton Ellis' infamous novel set in the late 80s, American Psycho centers on 
soulless, yuppie stockbroker Patrick Bateman (Bale), whose obsession with luxury goods and 
penchant for rape, torture, and murder lead him down a precarious, serial-killing path.
