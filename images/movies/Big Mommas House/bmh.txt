Big Momma's House  

Starring: Martin Lawrence, Nia Long and Paul Giamatti

Directed by: Raja Gosnell

Rating: NR    Parents' Rating Guide


SYNOPSIS: Martin Lawrence plays an undercover cop who is sent to Georgia to protect a single mother (Nia Long) and her son. He adopts the identity of a crass, heavy-set Southern grandmother known as "Big Momma" in order to hide his real identity.
