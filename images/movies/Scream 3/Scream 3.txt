Scream 3

Starring David Arquette, Neve Campbell and Courtney Cox
Directed by Wes Craven
Rated R
Running time: 116 min.

[Synopsis] 
When Sidney (Campbell) heads for Hollywood in the this third episode of
 the Scream trilogy, she is unable to escape her terrifying past. 
David Arquette, Courtney Cox and Liev Schreiber reprise roles and 
Parker Posey makes an appearance in the continuing story about a 
killer who is inspired by his love of horror movies.
