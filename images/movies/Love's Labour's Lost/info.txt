Love's Labour's Lost  
 
Friday, June 09, 2000  

 Starring: Nathan Lane, Alicia Silverstone, Alessandro Nivola and Kenneth Branagh

Directed by: Kenneth Branagh

Rating: PG    Parents' Rating Guide

Running Time: 93 min.

SYNOPSIS: In this updated version of Shakespeare’s play, the King of Navarre and three of his friends make a pact to devote the rest of their lives to scholarly study. But when a beautiful princess (Alicia Silverstone) comes to town, plans quickly change.
