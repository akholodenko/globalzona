Deuce Bigalow: Male Gigolo

Starring Rob Schneider, Arija Bareikis, Allen Covert and Eddie Griffin
Directed by Mike Mitchell VI
Rated NR

Synopsis 
--------
Fish tank cleaner Deuce Bigalow (Schneider) is not much of a ladies man
. But when he gets a house sitting/fish caring job from a high-class 
male gigolo, Deuce accidentally destroys his prize aquarium. Desperate 
to make money to replace it before his client returns, Deuce begins to 
answer his client's business calls.
