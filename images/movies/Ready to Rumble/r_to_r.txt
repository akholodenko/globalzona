Ready to Rumble

Starring David Arquette, John Goodman, Oliver Platt and Scott Caan
Directed by Brian Robbins
Rated PG-13
Running time: 107 min.

Synopsis
--------
When wrestler Jimmy "The King" King (Platt) loses a big match, two 
die-hard fans (Arquette and Caan) fear his career may be over. Hoping 
to help "The King" make a comeback, they head for Atlanta to register 
as a new tag-team, planning to fight him, lose terribly, and thus get 
Jimmy back in the spotlight.
