The Beach

Starring Leonardo DiCaprio and Virginie Ledoyen
Directed by Danny Boyle
Rated R
Running Time: 118 mi.

Synopsis 
Trainspotting director Danny Boyle's adaptation of Alex Garland's 
adventure novel about jaded Westerners trying to get away from it all. 
An American traveler Richard (DiCaprio) meets a thrill-hungry French 
couple who agrees to help him find a secret Thai beach they imagine to 
be paradise. But after barely surviving the trip, the trio encounters 
drug commandos and a bizarre commune of international drifters living 
in this supposed Eden.
