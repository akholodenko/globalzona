Keeping the Faith

Starring Ben Stiller, Edward Norton, Jenna Elfman and Kryss Anderson
Directed by Edward Norton
Rated PG-13


Synopsis 
--------
Actor Edward Norton makes his directorial debut in this romantic comedy focusing on the 
relationship between childhood friends -- Norton as a Catholic priest and Stiller as an 
orthodox rabbi -- who both fall in love with the same woman (Elfman) and begin to question 
their faith.
