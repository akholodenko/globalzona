Chicken Run

Starring: Mel Gibson, Julia Sawahla and Miranda Richardson

Directed by: Nick Park and Peter Lord

Rating: PG    Parents' Rating Guide

Running Time: 85 min.

SYNOPSIS: Directed by the Academy Award winning team of animators Nick Park and Peter Lord, Chicken Run tells the story of two chickens who fall in love on a British farm. Together, they attempt a daring escape from the clutches of their evil owner. Mel Gibson and Julia Sawahla are the voices of the two love birds.
