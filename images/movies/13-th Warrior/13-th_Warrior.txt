Synopsis
-------- 
Ibn Fadlan (Banderas), an Arab emissary in the year AD 922 is captured 
by a band of barbaric Vikings on their seasonal sacking, looting and 
pillaging tour of the Nordic coast. He soon learns he has been 
recruited to fight a legendary beast with an enormous appetite for 
human flesh. Based on Michael Crichton's story "Eaters of the Dead," 
which in turn is said to be based on the classic epic "Beowulf."
