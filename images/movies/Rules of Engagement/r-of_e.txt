Rules of Engagement

Starring Tommy Lee Jones, Ben Kingsley, Samuel L. Jackson and Anne Archer
Directed by William Friedkin
Rated R
Running time: 123 min.

Synopsis
--------
In this courtroom drama, Colonel Childers (Jackson) is charged with 
ordering his troops to fire against civilians during an attack on a 
foreign U.S. Embassy. Tommy Lee Jones plays the jaded military lawyer 
who must defend Childers, the man who saved his life during Vietnam.
