Return to Me

Starring David Duchovny, Minnie Driver and Carroll O'Connor
Directed by Bonnie Hunt
Rated PG
Running time: 116 min.

Synopsis
--------
A widower (Duchovny) finds true love after being set up on a blind date
 with a woman (Driver) who seems somehow familiar to him. After she 
confesses that she has had a heart transplant, they realize that her 
new heart once belonged to the widower's wife.
