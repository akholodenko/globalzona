Titan A.E.

Starring: Matt Damon, Bill Pullman, Hank Azaria and Drew Barrymore
Rating: PG    Parents' Rating Guide

SYNOPSIS: Far in the future (3028), Earth is destroyed by an alien race called the Drej. One teenager named Cale (the voice of Matt Damon) goes on a quest to find a mysterious "earthship" called The Titan which holds the key to saving the rest mankind from extinction.
