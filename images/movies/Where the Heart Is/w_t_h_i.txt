Where the Heart Is

Starring Natalie Portman, Ashley Judd, Joan Cusack and Sally Field
Directed by Matt Williams
Rated PG-13

Synopsis 
--------
An adaptation of the bestseller by Billie Letts in which pregnant 
young Novalee Nation (Portman) is dumped by her boyfriend at an 
Oklahoma Wal-Mart store and decides to take up residence and give 
birth there. There she finds a quirky set of characters who help 
her and her baby.
