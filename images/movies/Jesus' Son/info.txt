Jesus' Son

Starring: Billy Crudup, Samantha Morton, Jack Black and Dennis Hopper

Directed by: Allison Maclean

Rating: R    Parents' Rating Guide


SYNOPSIS: Based on a collection of short stories by Denis Johnson, this movie tells the story of one young man’s journey through the drug culture of the 1970s. Billy Crudup stars in this edgy tale that takes him from drug dependency and petty crime to redemption in the form of a job at a nursing home.
