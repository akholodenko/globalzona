Me, Myself, and Irene

Starring: Jim Carrey and Renee Zellweger

Directed by: Bobby Farrelly and Peter Farrelly

Rating: R    Parents' Rating Guide

Running Time: 116 min.

SYNOPSIS: Jim Carrey re-teams with the Farrelly brothers (Dumb and Dumber) in this romantic comedy about a Rhode Island trooper named Charlie who has a multiple personality disorder. When he forgets to take his medication, Charlie finds himself fighting over the same woman with his violent, sexually aggressive alter-ego, Hank.
