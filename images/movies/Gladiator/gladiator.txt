Gladiator

Starring Russell Crowe, Joaquin Phoenix, Richard Harris and Djimon Hounsou
Directed by Ridley Scott
Rated R
Running time: 150 min.

Synopsis
--------
Maximus (Crowe), once named to succeed Caesar, is exiled by the 
jealous heir to the throne, Commodus (Phoenix). Returning to Rome 
from a life of slavery, Maximus uses his training as a gladiator to 
challenge Commodus, avenge his family's murder, and win the admiration 
of the Roman people.
