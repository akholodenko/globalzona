Center Stage

Starring Amanda Schull and Peter Gallagher
Directed by Nicholas Hytner
Rated PG-13
Running time: 116 min.

Synopsis 
--------
A group of young dancers in New York pursue their dreams of becoming professional ballerinas despite the intense pressures and difficulties that lie in their paths. The Madness of King George director Nicholas Hynter showcases a cast of newcomers in this drama set in the dance world.
