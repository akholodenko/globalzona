Gladiator
Starring: Russell Crowe, Joaquin Phoenix, Richard Harris and Djimon Hounsou
Directed by: Hugh Hudson
Rated: PG-13
Running time: 120 min.
Synopsis
Maximus (Crowe), once named to succeed Caesar, is exiled by the jealous heir to the throne, Commodus (Phoenix). Returning to Rome from a life of slavery, Maximus uses his training as a gladiator to challenge Commodus, avenge his family's murder, and win the admiration of the Roman people.

I Dream of Africa
Starring: Kim Basinger, Vincent Perez and Allison Daugherty Smith
Directed by: Gregory Hoblit
Rated: PG-13
Running time: 117 min.
Synopsis
A wildlife preservation advocate (Basinger) moves back to Africa where she lived as a child and discovers not only the wild beauty of the country, but her own perseverance and tenacity in the face of tragedy.

Battlefield Earth
Starring: John Travolta, Barry Pepper, Forest Whitaker and Kelly Preston
Directed by: Roger Christian
Rated: PG-13
Running time: 117 min.
Synopsis
John Travolta stars in this sci-fi adaptation of an L. Ron Hubbard novel in which humans are forced underground after an alien invasion. The ten-foot aliens (led by Travolta as their overlord) oppress the weaker humans, who fight valiantly against them.

Screwed
Starring: Norm MacDonald, Danny DeVito, Elaine Stritch and David Chappelle
Directed by: Scott Alexander and Larry Karaszewski
Rated: PG-13
Running time: 90 min.
Synopsis
Willard Fillmore is a disgruntled chauffeur who cooks up a scheme to get back at his abusive boss, Miss Crock. Willard ropes in his best friend and a mortician and together they kidnap the boss' dog. The plan goes awry when the dog escapes and Miss Crock thinks her chauffeur is the one who's been kidnapped
