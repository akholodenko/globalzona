The Bachelor
Starring: Chris O'Donnel, N/A
Directed by: N/A
Rated: N/A
Running time: N/A
Synopsis
A remake of the Buster Keaton film Seven Chances, The Bachelor tells the story of Jimmie Shannon (O'Donnell), a confirmed bachelor who suddenly stands to inherit $100 million, provided he is married before his next birthday, which unfortunately happens to be in twenty-four hours. His ex-girlfriend (Shields), along with at least a thousand other women dressed in white, answer his ad in the newspaper asking to meet him at the wedding chapel, and general mayhem ensues.
Three Kings
Starring: George Clooney, Mark Wahlberg and Ice Cube
Directed by: David O. Russell
Rated: R
Running time: 115 min.
Synopsis
When the Gulf War ends, American soldiers Archie Gates (Clooney), Troy Barlow (Wahlberg) and Chief Elgin (Cube) decide to stay in Iraq to search for a cache of gold Saddam Husein was said to have stolen from Kuwait. Relying on a map that marks the location of the desert bunker where the gold supposedly is, the three men set out to find it and get the reward they feel they deserve. The soldiers let nothing stand in their way of their new capitalistic mission, fighting off any resistance they encounter.
American Beauty
Starring: Kevin Spacey, Annette Bening and Thora Birch
Directed by: Sam Mendes
Rated: R
Running time: 122 min.
Synopsis
Television writer Alan Ball's first produced screenplay introduces us to the chaotic world found on the tree-lined street of Robin Hood Trail, Anytown, U.S.A. Provoked by forbidden passions, Lester Burnham (Kevin Spacey) decides to make a few changes in his rut of a life changes that are less midlife crisis than adolescence reborn. The freer he gets, the happier he gets, which is even more maddening to his wife, Carolyn (Annette Bening), and daughter Jane (Thora Birch)-- especially when he turns his lustful gaze toward Jane's friend, the sultry Angela (Mena Suvari). Carolyn responds by focusing her attention on real estate colleague Buddy Kane (Peter Gallagher).
The 13th Warrior
Starring: Antonio Banderas, Diane Venora and Omar Sharif
Directed by: Michael Crichton and John McTierman
Rated: R
Running time: 114 min.
Synopsis
Ibn Fadlan (Banderas), an Arab emissary in the year AD 922 is captured by a band of barbaric Vikings on their seasonal sacking, looting and pillaging tour of the Nordic coast. He soon learns he has been recruited to fight a legendary beast with an enormous appetite for human flesh. Based on Michael Crichton's story "Eaters of the Dead," which in turn is said to be based on the classic epic "Beowulf."
