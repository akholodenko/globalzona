Rules of Engagement
Starring: Tommy Lee Jones, Ben Kingsley, Samuel L. Jackson and Anne Archer
Directed by: William Friedkin
Rated: R
Running time: 123 min.
Synopsis
In this courtroom drama, Colonel Childers (Jackson) is charged with ordering his troops to fire against civilians during an attack on a foreign U.S. Embassy. Tommy Lee Jones plays the jaded military lawyer who must defend Childers, the man who saved his life during Vietnam.

Ready to Rumble
Starring: David Arquette, John Goodman, Oliver Platt and Scott Caan
Directed by: Brian Robbins
Rated: PG-13
Running time: 107 min.
Synopsis
When wrestler Jimmy "The King" King (Platt) loses a big match, two die-hard fans (Arquette and Caan) fear his career may be over. Hoping to help "The King" make a comeback, they head for Atlanta to register as a new tag-team, planning to fight him, lose terribly, and thus get Jimmy back in the spotlight.

Return to Me
Starring:David Duchovny, Minnie Driver and Carroll O'Connor
Directed by: Bonnie Hunt
Rated: PG
Running time: 116 min.
Synopsis
A widower (Duchovny) finds true love after being set up on a blind date with a woman (Driver) who seems somehow familiar to him. After she confesses that she has had a heart transplant, they realize that her new heart once belonged to the widower's wife.

High Fidelity
Starring: John Cusack, Iben Hjejle, Lisa Bonet and Tim Robbins
Directed by: Stephen Frears
Rated: R
Running time: 107 min.
Synopsis
Adapted from the popular Nick Hornby novel, this is the story of a thirty-something record store owner (Cusack) who is forced to confront his arrested adolescence when his girlfriend (Hjejle) dumps him.
