The Ninth Gate
Starring: Johnny Depp, Frank Langella and Lena Olin
Directed by:Roman Polanski
Rated: R
Running time: 127 min.
Synopsis
Dean Corso (Depp) is a rare book tracker hired by European book collector and professor of demonology Boris Balkan (Langella) to find the remaining copies of a 17th-century series entitled The Nine Gates of the Shadow Kingdom. As his quest takes him from New York to Paris to Madrid, Corso begins to suspect the motives behind his assignment when he learns the books are said to be capable of summoning Satan.
The Next Big Thing
Starring: Madonna and Rupert Everett
Directed by: John Schlesinger
Rated: PG-13
Running time: N/A min.
Synopsis
When Abbie (Madonna) and her gay best friend Robert (Everett) decide to raise a baby together, neither is prepared for how the experience will affect their relationship. When Abbie falls for another man (Benjamin Bratt), Robert won't give up his claim on the child and the two end up in a custody battle in this comedy from veteran director John Schlesinger.

Pitch Black
Starring: Cole Hauser, Vin Diesel and Radha Mitchell
Directed by: David Twohy
Rated: R
Running time: 107 min.
Synopsis
When a damaged ship transporting passengers is forced to crash-land, the survivors end up on a seemingly lifeless planet. But when an eclipse throws them into complete darkness, the planet suddenly erupts with vicious, deadly creatures. Together, they must race from the crash site through a steep, treacherous canyon to escape these nocturnal aliens and reach the ship alive.

The Beach
Starring: Leonardo DiCaprio and Virginie Ledoyen
Directed by: Danny Boyle
Rated: R
Running time: 118 min.
Synopsis
Trainspotting director Danny Boyle's adaptation of Alex Garland's adventure novel about jaded Westerners trying to get away from it all. An American traveler Richard (DiCaprio) meets a thrill-hungry French couple who agrees to help him find a secret Thai beach they imagine to be paradise. But after barely surviving the trip, the trio encounters drug commandos and a bizarre commune of international drifters living in this supposed Eden.
