American Psycho
Starring: Christian Bale, Willem Dafoe, Jared Leto and Chloe Sevigny
Directed by: Mary Harron
Rated: R
Running time: 97 min.
Synopsis
Based on Bret Easton Ellis' infamous novel set in the late 80s, American Psycho centers on soulless, yuppie stockbroker Patrick Bateman (Bale), whose obsession with luxury goods and penchant for rape, torture, and murder lead him down a precarious, serial-killing path.

Keeping the Faith
Starring:Ben Stiller, Edward Norton, Jenna Elfman and Kryss Anderson
Directed by: Edward Norton
Rated: PG-13
Running time: N/A min.
Synopsis
Actor Edward Norton makes his directorial debut in this romantic comedy focusing on the relationship between childhood friends -- Norton as a Catholic priest and Stiller as an orthodox rabbi -- who both fall in love with the same woman (Elfman) and begin to question their faith.

Love & Basketball
Starring: Ben Sanaa Lathan, Omar Epps and Alfre Woodard
Directed by: Gina Prince
Rated: R
Running time: 124 min.
Synopsis
Two ambitious basketball players (Lathan and Epps) share a volatile relationship on and off the court. Stars of their respective teams, the childhood sweethearts struggle to grow up, play the game, and stay together in this romantic comedy by first-time director Gina Prince.

Gossip
Starring: James Marsden, Norman Reedus and Joshua Jackson
Directed by: Davis Guggenheim
Rated: R
Running time: N/A min.
Synopsis
A group of college students plant a rumor and create a web of gossip which spins so out of control that one man is falsely accused of a crime and a woman kills herself. Marsden stars as a pre-law student and Reedus as his admiring friend.
