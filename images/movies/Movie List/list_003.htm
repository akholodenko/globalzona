Magnolia
Starring: William H. Macy, Julianne Moore, Jason Robards and Tom Cruise
Directed by: Paul Thomas Anderson
Rated: R
Running time: 188 min.
Synopsis
Already being called an "instant classic" by those lucky enough to have seen a sneak preview, P. T. Anderson's Magnolia is one of the most anticipated movies of the holiday season. After his tour de force Boogie Nights it's not difficult to imagine why. But maybe it has something to do with the Robert Altman-esque type trailer that recently hit theaters or the vague descriptions that have been leaked to the masses. But from what we know we think it goes something like this: the movie takes place in one day in the San Fernando Valley and it's an ensemble piece which loosely revolves around the core relationships of an ailing father (Jason Robards), his young wife (Julianne Moore), and his "lost" son (Tom Cruise). The cast also includes some of P. T.'s old favorites like John C. Reilly and Philip Seymour Hoffman.

Galaxy Quest
Starring: Tim Allen, Sigourney Weaver, Tony Shalhoub and Alan Rickman
Directed by: Dean Parisot
Rated: PG
Running time: 102 min.
Synopsis
In the 1970's Galaxy Quest was a popular outer-space television series, following the adventures of Commander Peter Quincy Taggart (Allen), Lieutenant Tawny Madison (Weaver) and their crew aboard the NSEA Protector. Twenty-five years later, the five actors are making an appearance at a sci-fi convention when they are kidnapped by aliens who believe them to be the space crusaders they portrayed on TV.

Bicentennial Man
Starring: Robin Williams, Embeth Davidtz, Oliver Platt and Sam Neill
Directed by: Chris Columbus
Rated: PG
Running time: 131 min.
Synopsis
Android Andrew (Williams) discovers his human side when he's bought by the Martin family to help out around the house. In a story that spans two centuries, Andrew learns about life, love, and the dangers of rust with the help of the Martins and their precocious daughter (Hallie Eisenberg, that cute Pepsi girl). Bicentennial Man is the second collaboration between Williams and director Chris Columbus, who previously teamed up for the wildly successful Mrs. Doubtfire. The pair hopes that Williams in a full-body robot suit will bring the same box office success as Williams in a wig and heels.

Deuce Bigalow: Male Gigolo
Starring: Rob Schneider, Arija Bareikis, Allen Covert and Eddie Griffin
Directed by: Mike Mitchell VI
Rated: NR
Running time: N/A
Synopsis
Fish tank cleaner Deuce Bigalow (Schneider) is not much of a ladies man . But when he gets a house sitting/fish caring job from a high-class male gigolo, Deuce accidentally destroys his prize aquarium. Desperate to make money to replace it before his client returns, Deuce begins to answer his client's business calls.
