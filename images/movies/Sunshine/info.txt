Sunshine

Starring: Ralph Fiennes and Rosemary Harris

Directed by: Istvan Szabo

Rating: R    Parents' Rating Guide

Running Time: 180 min.

SYNOPSIS: A century of political and social upheaval in Hungary is depicted in three generations, with Ralph Fiennes playing three different members of the same family -- a Jewish lawyer in 1900, an Olympic fencer during the 1930s, and a political activist in the early 1950s
