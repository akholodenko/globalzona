Erin Brockovich

Starring Julia Roberts, Albert Finney and Aaron Eckhart
Directed by Steven Soderbergh
Rated R
Running time: 130 min.

Synopsis
--------
Julia Roberts plays the title character in this drama about a file 
clerk who discovers a cover-up involving a contaminated local water 
supply. She must convince her boss and the residents of her town to 
let her investigate and go to court against the responsible company.
