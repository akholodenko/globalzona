The Virgin Suicides

Starring James Woods, Kathleen Turner, Danny DeVito and Giovanni Ribisi
Directed by Sofia Coppola
Rated R
Running time: 96 min.

Synopsis 
--------
Set in the suburban '70s, this seriocomic adaptation of the Jeffrey 
Eugenides novel tells the story of the mysterious, alluring, and 
tragic Lisbon girls as remembered by a group of neighborhood boys who 
loved them from afar. Sofia Coppola makes her directorial debut with 
this Sundance premiere.
