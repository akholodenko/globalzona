Groove
 
Friday, June 09, 2000  

Starring: Lola Glaudini and Hamish Linklater

Directed by: Gregory Harrison

Rating: R    Parents' Rating Guide

Running Time: 86 min.

SYNOPSIS: This low-budget indie movie stood out at this year’s Sundance film festival as one of the only movies that sold. The story takes place over the course of one night at a San Francisco rave, as a lonely young writer discovers love, purpose, and family secrets at the all night party.
