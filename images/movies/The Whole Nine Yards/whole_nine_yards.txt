The Whole Nine Yards

Starring Bruce Willis, Matthew Perry and Rosanna Arquette
Directed by Jonathan Lynn
Rated R
Running time: 103 min.

Synopsis
--------
Another mob movie by My Cousin Vinny director Jonathan Lynn. In this 
one, Bruce Willis plays Jimmy, a hit man with a heart of gold who's 
ratted out by his neighbor, Oz (Perry). But Oz finds himself in over 
his head when he finds himself set up by his own wife and in love with 
Jimmy?s (Natasha Henstridge).
