Screwed

Starring Norm MacDonald, Danny DeVito, Elaine Stritch and David Chappelle
Directed by Scott Alexander and Larry Karaszewski
Rated PG-13
Running time: 90 min.

Synopsis 
--------
Synopsis 
Willard Fillmore is a disgruntled chauffeur who cooks up a scheme to get back at his abusive boss, Miss Crock. Willard ropes in his best friend and a mortician and together they kidnap the boss' dog. The plan goes awry when the dog escapes and Miss Crock thinks her chauffeur is the one who's been kidnapped
