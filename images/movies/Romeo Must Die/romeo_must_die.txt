Romeo Must Die

Starring Jet Li and Isaiah Washington
Directed by Andrzej Bartkowiak
Rated R
Running time: 110 min.

Synopsis
--------
In this action drama starring Jet Li, two star-crossed lovers are kept 
apart by their rival crime families a la Romeo and Juliet. Li falls in 
love with the daughter of an enemy boss in the course of avenging his 
brother's death.
