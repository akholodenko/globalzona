Luminous Motion

Starring Eric Lloyd and Deborah Unger
Directed by Bette Gordon
Rated NA
Running time: 94 min.

Synopsis
--------
A woman (Unger) travels across country with her 10 year-old son, paying for the trip by selling her body along the way. When their car breaks down mid-trip, she falls in love with the mechanic who fixes it. Meanwhile, her son lives in a fantasy world of his own. The movie is based on the cult novel by the same name.
