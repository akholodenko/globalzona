Love and Basketball

Starring Sanaa Lathan, Omar Epps and Alfre Woodard
Directed by Gina Prince
Rated R
Running time: 124 min.

Synopsis 
--------
Two ambitious basketball players (Lathan and Epps) share a volatile relationship on and off the court. Stars of their respective teams, the childhood sweethearts struggle to grow up, play the game, and stay together in this romantic comedy by first-time director Gina Prince
