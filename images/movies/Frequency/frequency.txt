Frequency

Starring Dennis Quaid, Jim Caviezel and Andre Braugher
Directed by Gregory Hoblit
Rated PG-13
Running time: 117 min.


Synopsis 
--------
Jim Caviezel plays a homicide detective who figures out that he can 
communicate with his dead father and time travel via short-wave radio. 
Going back in time to talk to his dad a few weeks before his death in
 a fire, the detective is able to prevent a murder.
