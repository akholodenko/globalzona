Synopsis
--------
When Andy, the boy who owns a roomful of living toys, goes off to 
cowboy camp, an obsessive toy collector kidnaps Woody (Hanks) to add 
to his valuable collection. Spaceman Buzz Lightyear (Allen) organizes 
the rescue mission, taking along an army of assorted toys who must 
work together to save their friend and return home before Andy does.
