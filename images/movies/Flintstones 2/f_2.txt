The Flintstones in Viva Rock Vegas

Starring Mark Addy, Stephen Baldwin, Joan Collins and Alan Cumming
Directed by Brian Levant
Rated PG
Running time: 91 min.

vivarockvegas.com
Synopsis 
--------
This live action prequel to 1994's The Flintstones goes back in time 
to tell the story of Fred Flintstone's youth. Fred, who is from "the 
wrong side of the rocks," courts Wilma by taking her to Rock Vegas for 
a romantic weekend. They are joined by Barney Rubble and fiancee Betty.
