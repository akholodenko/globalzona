FORMED: 1993, Berlin, Germany

A trio of Olaf Jeglitza, Patricia Petersen and Vanessa Mason, the Real 
McCoy formed in Berlin in 1993 and soon became popular throughout 
Europe with the chart-topping hit "Another Night." In late 1994, the 
single hit number three on the American charts, as did the following 
year's "Run Away." Their debut album Another Night went double-platinum
 by late 1995. One More Time followed in 1997. 
~ John Bush, All-Music Guide
