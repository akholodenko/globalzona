Another Big Split
::02::16::01::
Another power house couple calls it quits. Following in the foot steps of both Tom and Nicole, famous Bad Boy producer and performer Sean " Puffy " Combs and actress/singer Jennifer Lopez are also wrapping up their relationship. After dating for over a year and going through numerous legal battles regarding with Puffy's gun shooting in a New York city night club they decided that it is better to discontinue their romantic encounter. The stock market is going down, people are breaking up around Valentine's Day, what's next?
The Big Split
::02::07::01::
One of the most promising relationships in Hollywood is coming to an end. Just two days after announcing their split, Tom Cruise and Nicole Kidman are headed for divorce court. Cruise has filed for divorce in Los Angeles Superior Court, citing irreconcilable differences. In addition, he is requesting joint custody of the couple's two adopted kids, Isabella Jane, 8, and Connor Anthony, 6, according to court papers. Now if these two love birds can't split a nest, how re the rest of us suppose to fall in line.
