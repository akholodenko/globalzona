Rushing Out Again
::01::18::01::
Surprising as it was, the 1998 action feature "Rush Hour", which stared the interracial duo of Chris Tucker and Jackie Chan did very well in the box office, breaking any previous doubts of films having nonwhite lead roles not having the ability to succeed. Currently, a second installment of the feature is in the works, with both lead characters coming back to claim their own. "Rush Hour 2" promises to be jam-packed with flying, kicking, and of course joking!

Britney Goes Hollywood
::01::12::01::
She has climbed the highest mountain of the music business and now Britney Spears is going to make her feature acting debut in a yet-to-be-titled coming-of-age story for director Tamra Davis. The popster will play a straight-A student, who travels cross-country with a group of her friends and meets a young musician who persuades the youths to come to Los Angeles to compete in a local musical contest. How well will Britney do? we just have to wait and see, but from the lip-singing, she'll probably do well!
