43rd Grammy Award Show
::02::22::01::
The 43rd Annual Grammy Award show was telecasted by CBS last night. The broadcast included presentations by insteresting people and performances by talented artists. However, the host of the show was not the best man for the job. While the jokes were endless, the humor in them could not be seen even through a miscroscope. But, showstoppers like Toni Braxton with her strap-on dress took the attention away from the negative aspects and turned hte eyes of the audience toward the stage and music. The Tranny
::02::14::01::
RuPaul plans to follow in the high-heel footsteps of Fran Drescher with his new show, The Tranny. The pilot focuses on the tall drag queen as a show-biz hopeful who pays the bills by helping to raise three kids. Oddly enough, RuPaul plays the most normal member of an otherwise dysfunctional family. The show has been picked up by UPN for a fall premiere.
