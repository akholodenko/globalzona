Journal Entry
06.06.00
I'm back and better than ever, or so I think. I'm kind of looking forward to tomorrow, although I think I'm going to regret saying that later. The reason why is because I'm going back to work after two weeks tomorrow. My boss has been out of town and I haven't worked since then. I've been kind of bored lately and really wanted to do something. But, when I start, it's probably going to be boring and I will only think of the money and when it will be over. One way or another, my time will not be wasted.

Got a reply from Questus.com today. Aparently they don't need any HTML coders now and if something opens up will get back to me. Whatever. I went through a lot of the listings at Guru.com and MonsterBoard.com and applied for a couple of jobs. Hopefully they will reply.

I'm also planning to ask for a raise at my current job. Since before there were two people working on the same level, myself and a co-workers, and now one quit due to residence relocation, I will be doing her share of work too. I think it's just fair. I'm not going to quit just because I don't get the raise, but I will know how much my boss appreciates me. Hopefully he does.

I am taking a sumer school class this time around. And as many of you might think, no, I didn't flunk a class last semester. I want to take this class ahead of time so I have room for this extra class I want to take. American Democracy will be dominating summer, allowing room for a full - year class of Advanced Placement Economics. Notes or whatever I can dig up from both classed will be posted in the School Help section, along with the already present information.
While I am one to go out and have a good time, I am also one to relax and rest. Yesterday I went bike riding at the "boardwalk" of Ocean Beach, which is a cemented area on the beach where one can walk, skate, etc. I was right there for the sunset and it was beautiful. I then rode up to this big hill from a cemented side and went down on the dune - sand side, sliding all over the place. It was so much fun.

San Francisco is not just a city that should be enjoyed by tourists, locals should also take advantage of all of the fastivities; there is so much to see. The outdoors await visitors.
