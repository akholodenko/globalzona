Music: Dre Gives List 05.25.00
Once again, following in the steps of his collegues, rapper - producer Dr. Dre did exactly what Metallica did a couple of weeks ago. The celebrity provided the online music distributor, Napster, with his own list of the company's software users who have download downloaded his musical products.

In light of the thousands of users being banned from accessing the Napster software, hackers have discovered alternative ways of getting around the ban. So far, over 350,000 members have been banned, especially ones in school campuses.

Music: Napster Gains More Support 05.02.00
Unlike the recent news circling the music entertainment industry, the band " Offspring " will not be filing a lawsuit against the online music distributor, Napster. In actuality, the band announced that they have completely nothing against the company or their product, and actually support it, saying the free distribution increases the popularity of the band's music.
