Biography
The Early Years
I was born Dec. 29, 1974, in Eugene, Oregon. I grew up there in the mid-sized city of Eugene in a large family--six kids. My early memories include reading voraciously and enjoying school. For several years I wore glasses, which I despised, and this combined with my blossoming intellectualism earned me the label of "nerd". Fortunately, I survived these early years and can look back and laugh. My father was a firm believer in family fun and took us 3-wheeling on the Oregon dunes, snow-skiing, and rafting. We had a ton of chores, though, and fought frequently! Now we are all great friends.

Becoming
Somehow, during high school, I grew out of the insecure nerd mode and even became a cheerleader. It was great fun but I felt strange when I was taller than even some of the basketball players! Though still a little nerdy in my own way, I had plenty of friends and fun times. At 14, I was approached by a fashion photographer and introduced to the world of modeling. But my parents were wary of the business and I was not really ready to pursue it. So while the seed was planted, it was several years and 3 inches later that I gave it another try.

New Direction
From the time I was just four years old, I wanted to be a teacher. My interest turned toward math during high school, so I hit the books and earned my B.A. in mathematics followed by a teaching credential. But life took a detour and I decided to give modeling a try. I finished college without becoming truly computer-literate, so website building and promotion was a real challenge. But the whole idea seemed more exciting than tracking satellites, joining some think tank or teaching high schoolers. So far it has been well worth it, and I do plan to use my education someday.

Arrival
This modeling adventure has certainly brought unique people and experiences into my life. It also has provided an outlet for another side of me-- sort of a cross between the actress and the tigress. I'm pretty down-to-earth, so presenting certain images and emotions can be quite a stretch. This business requires serious inner strength and determination as well as a true sense of self. With so much focus on the physical image, one must radiate confidence, uninhibition and true character. My ultimate goals have yet to be set in this business, but I will say that I am not satisfied with mediocrity. I create my own definition of success, so be watching for me!
