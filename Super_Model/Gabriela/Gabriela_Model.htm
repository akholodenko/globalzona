Gabriela

Biography
Gabriela was born a 4 of April in Rosario´is City, Santa Fe, Argentina. She is the older of two sisters. She grew up in a pretty big family.

When she was little she spent her summers in the Bancario's Club. There she started to practice sports. She swam many years, amateur and professionally , she whom some medals. She also did canopy with her father, learn tennis, basket and volley. And was with the volley that she found her self, she began federate and took part of a team with whom she played professionally some seasons. But that was not her destiny yet, She also took some ballet classes and act in The Circulo´s theater, and after that she left.

It was when the volley team got dissolved that she had to choose her way.
Very tall and skinny as few girls of her age, his friends gave her an advice:be a ballet dancer or a model. Following their advice she took a model course and even before finishing it she began to work. She did dome shows but it was in the Melocoton show that she met Pancho Dotto (a models manager) who was in Rosario with some girls of his staff as Araceli Gonzales, Elizabeth Marquez, Valeria Mazza and others. Elizabeth Marquez one of his girls was who first ask Gabriela if she wanted to work in Buenos Aires as a model and introduce Pancho Dotto to her who ask her the same question.

In that show she also had the opportunity to send photographic material to the Look of the year contest that took place in Buenos Aires a few months later. In that opportunity she and her mother had a talk with Pancho Dotto, who ask her again if she wanted to work in Buenos Aires and gave her family the necessary relief to let her daughter go to live in the capital city.

And was like that that Gabriela ended college and in February of the next year made her luggage and travel to Buenos Aires together with another model called Silvina Barrera. Both arrived in Buenos Aires to stay a week as a prove and never come back. At the very beginning she lived in the Pancho Dotto´s agency apartment which made things easier, there she made new friends and learn everything about the models world. She lived in different moments with Catalina Rautemberg, Maria Ines Rivero, Laura Tuells, Alejandra Puig, Paula Medici, Silvina Barrera, Lorena Garcia and Soledad Rodriguez.

It was with Pancho Dotto that she began her model career, with his agency she spent the summer season in Punta del Este, create her first book and made her first important jobs, one of the the one she counts as the travel of her life, a fashion production for the Especial of moda magazine and because of that job she had travel 15 days to Egypt, "the most wonderful place on earth".That trip also brought to her her first cover and lots of new material for her book. It was after that trip that she decided to make a change in her career and concerted and interview with another models manager Ricardo Piñeiro who in the first interview gave her the contract to sign and with whom she actually works.

Once in Ricardo´s agency she start to grew as a woman and as a model, after six months she made all the fashion shows. With only 17 years old she shared the shows with Argentinean top models as Teresita Garbesi, Mariana Arias, Elizabeth Marquez, Carolina Peleritti and a lot more. And the years went by and brought a lot of other works, between them she had the campaigns of Chocolate, Sail, Vanity Fair and more. The covers and the fashion productions of the magazines: Elle, Para Ti, Plena, D&D, Cosmopolitan, BA colecciones, Telva, Caras and Gente.

She also spent a season in the city of Milan when she had the chance to meet Giorgio Armani and to get even more experience. She´s actually working in Argentina.

At this moment May of 1999 she´s doing an Italian fashion week show with designers as Fendi, Fendissima, La Perla and others.

August 1999- Gabriela got back from Las Leñas where she made de Tia María´s show. She also already did the shows of Maria Pryor, Escada, Laurel and she´s getting ready to so the season shows of Grandes Colecciones and Alta moda..
