Tracie

Biography
My full name is Tracie Andrea Tyler.

I was born in Milwaukee, WI at St Joseph's Hospital at 10 to midnight. Hence, I am a night owl. I am the youngest of three daughters and a Virgo (with Virgo rising). I have a bachelor's degree in Sociology and minored in American History. I am African, Cuban, Powhatan, Choctaw and English. John Tyler, the 10th president of the United States, is my great great great grandfather. How cool is that?

I was raised primarily on the East Coast in New Hampshire and New Jersey but spent my high school years in Milwaukee. While there, I attended Jean Nicolet High School (I think Oprah went there). One summer vacation I visited California and decided that after high school that it was where I wanted to be. After graduation I returned and I'm still here. I began modeling around that time but did not take it seriously until I arrived in Los Angeles. Since '91 I have modeled professionally and love it. I would have it no other way.

A few things about me:

I adore: the sun, a great sense of humor, long showers, waking up to the sun, sleeping late, people with integrity, dining out, the beach, shopping, dogs, sweets, neatness (without being anal) and men who love to cook.

I hate: crowded places, loud people, hospitals, being rushed, smog, rudeness, long airplane flights, being cold, stress, the dark, bad cooking, bugs and anything remotely reptilian. Ack!

While I've modeled I've been very blessed to have had many great experiences and opportunities. I hope they will continue. I will probably model for a few more years and follow the path it has created wherever it may lead me (hopefully to a TV show or feature film!) Eventually, I would like to settle down and have a family because I'm just a lil 'ol fashioned farm girl at heart... Well not really, but sort of.
