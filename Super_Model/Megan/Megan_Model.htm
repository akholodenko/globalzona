Interview
Name
Megan Seely

Age
18

Marital status
single

Hometown
Vancouver, BC, Canada

Education-where/when/level
I'm currently enrolled in high school in Grade 12
Completed John Casablancas Fashion Institute - Teen Modelling course

Present job
high school student, some local acting and modelling

Goals for future
-to have a career that I am happy and comfortable in
-to have enough free time to enjoy travelling and art

What got you interested in modelling?
Basically I had people around me telling me I should do modelling. I took a modelling course and was scouted not long afterwards.

How old were you?
16

What experience, if any, do you have modelling?
catalogue work for local designers
hair modelling for Sebastian and Matrix products
print work in Healing Retreats and Spas magazine
local fashion shows
working with local photographers

What kind of modelling do you see yourself doing?
I'd like to do more editorial-based stuff, and international print and magazine work

Is there anything about the modelling profession that scares you?
Being caught up in a scam of some sort, being told I will do one thing but have to do another, basically I don't want to be put in a position where I'm doing something I would rather not simply because I signed a contract or accepted a job.

What is your strongest feature?
I've been told my eyes a lot.

How do you take care of your body? complexion?
I walk pratically everywhere instead of driving or taking the bus. I also try not to use much makeup outside of modelling, and use sensitive skin
products.

How do you feel in front of the camera?
I've been told that I am comfortable by many photographers.

What physical activities does your typical day consist of?
Mainly walking

Do you consider yourself a healthy eater?
I'm a moderately healthy eater. I'm not a health food freak but I don't eat
junk food much either.

What is your favorite food to binge on?
Chocolate ice cream

What do you hope to achieve by modelling?
I don't want to model all my life, but it would be an easy way for me to earn spare change until I decide what I want to do with my life.

Who is your role model (doesn't have to be a model)
I don't really have a role model, it's more like I can look up to certain aspects of different people rather than idolize just one person.

Which supermodel do you look up to the most?
Claudia Schiffer- basically because we have the same birthday

Would you ever pose in the nude?
No.

What, if anything, could persuade you to pose nude?
Nothing.

What would your family think of this choice?
They would agree with whatever I decided. But I think they'd prefer that I chose not to pose nude.

What is the best beauty secret you know of that you utilize?
Not blow drying or dyeing my hair. I damaged my hair a while ago quite badly from excessive heat and dye, and it all had to be cut off. So I swore than to leave it in a natural state.

Are you willing to travel?
I'd like to, providing all expenses are paid and it doesn't interfere with my schoolwork.

Are you willing to possibly relocate?
I'd consider relocating once I graduated, when I'm in school it's not really an issue.

If you had your choice of where to live, where would you choose?
I'd prefer to be in a city (i'm a city girl) in North America or in Europe.
