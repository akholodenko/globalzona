Traci

Interview
Name
Traci Hitchcox

Age
21

Marital status
Married

Hometown
St. Pete., FL

Education-where/when/level
I attended high school at Northeast High School in St. Pete., FL and graduated in '96. I attended St. Pete. Junior College for 2 years but did not obtain a degree. I still attend St. Pete. Junior College, takimg a couple classes a year.

Present job
I work at a Christian Pre-School as a teacher's assistant. The school houses 2-5 year olds.

Goals for future
My goal for the future is to model full-time and have a successful modeling career for as long as I can.

What got you interested in modelling?
I have always been interested in modeling. Since I was very young, I was always interested in fashion and loved to look at fashion magazines and try on all types of clothes.

How old were you?
I started modeling when I was 19.

What experience, if any, do you have modelling?
Featured model at the runway/hair show for Farouk Systems.
Model in the hair show for Wella.
Featured swimsuit model in Exotic Bikini swimsuit magazine.
Model for the Safety Harbour Resort and Spa brochures and other publications.
Swimsuit model for Skiwear.com.
Model for advertisement for Susan Jeffers photography.
Featured model for March's issue of the online magazine INDUSTRY and also appearing in Feb.'s issue.

What kind of modelling do you see yourself doing?
Runway, commercial print, editorial, print, fashion, catalog.

Is there anything about the modelling profession that scares you?
There is nothing really that scares me about the modelling profession. It's all what you put into it.

What is your strongest feature?
My strongest feature is my eyes. They are very blue. When I show people my pictures, they usually think my eyes have been colored. Also, I have extremely long eyelashes.

How do you take care of your body? complexion?
I think it's very important to take care of yourself. I stay very fit by running everyday and doing light excercises, and eating healthy foods. Your diet plays a very important role on how your body and skin looks. I stay away from fatty, greasy foods and drink plenty of water. For my skin, I use an exfoliating soap and use day and night creams by RIVAGE.

How do you feel in front of the camera?
I feel very comfortable in front of the camera. A good part of my life was spent taking ballet. I took a total of 15 years, beginning from the time I was 2. I participated in many recitals throughout these years in front of hundreds of people. I have never been afraid to be in front of people.

What physical activities does your typical day consist of?
I work at a pre-school, so my day consists of picking up kids all day long, running around with them and chasing them on the playground. When I get home, I go for a run and work out for a little bit.

Do you consider yourself a healthy eater?
I consider myself a healthy eater. I have been a vegetarian since I was 12. The past year, I have included chicken in my diet. I love vegetables, fruit, pastas, but I also love my chocolate.

What is your favorite food to binge on?
Any kind of chocolate.

What do you hope to achieve by modelling?
Through modelling, I hope to travel the world and just have fun.

Who is your role model (doesn't have to be a model)
I think my role model is Kate Moss. Because like her, I am not as tall as the industry would like me to be and she has acheived so much. She has mastered every aspect of the modeling business, from runway to commercials. I hope to be as succesful as her.

Which supermodel do you look up to the most?
I think I look up to Kate Moss the most. Because she has acheived great success despite her short height for a model. She has done print work, commercial and even runway. She has done it all and it shows girls that aren't 5' 10 that they can do anything they want too.

Would you ever pose in the nude?
I would never pose in the nude.

What, if anything, could persuade you to pose nude?
Nothing could persuade me to pose nude. Not for any amount of money. I was brought up that way. There is nothing that needs to have a naked body to sell it. I believe the human body is beautiful, but I'm not interested in that part of the industry.

What would your family think of this choice?
They would support me in this decision.

Are you willing to travel?
I am willing to travel anywhere in the world.

Are you willing to possibly relocate?
I would relocate anywhere in the world that would help me acheive my dream to become a successful model.

If you had your choice of where to live, where would you choose?
If I had my choice of where to live, I would probably pick California. I love the people there, the weather is perfect(in certain spots). I love that you can drive one way and it's desert. Drive another way and it's snowing. It's just so different everywhere you go. But most of all, I love the way it makes you feel when you're there.
