Amber

Interview
Name
Amber Gangi

Age
22

Marital status
Engaged

Hometown
Fredericksburg, VA

Education-where/when/level
I graduated in 1996 from Orange County High School.

Present job
I work for an animal hospital as a veterinarian technician.

Goals for future
I simply hope to succeed in all that I put my mind to.

What got you interested in modelling?
I was not the typical little girl that dreamed of becoming a model when I grew up. In fact I was much the tom-boy and cared nothing for beauty. Modeling was something that found me and I then pursued it.

How old were you?
I was 15 when I was first approached by a modeling scout.

What experience, if any, do you have modelling?
I cannot say I know everything about the modeling business - in fact I still have much to learn. I only model on a part time basis and have gained my knowledge through those that I was fortunate enough to work with and learn from.

What kind of modelling do you see yourself doing?
With all the different types of modeling I have experienced, I enjoy runway the most. I like being on stage and love to meet and work with many people.

Is there anything about the modelling profession that scares you?
Absolutely! If I were to say no I would be lying to myself. When I take a job I always like to obtain references and then thoroughly check them. As a freelance model, one can never tell how trustworthy or honest the person she's about to work for truly is.

What is your strongest feature?
In front of a camera I have been told that my strongest feature is my eyes. This of course was not a gift handed to me but something that I had to learn to use when I was in front of the camera. That is to say, I have to make a conscious effort to make my eyes appear "just right," but when I'm successful, the results are usually great.

How do you take care of your body? complexion?
First, my job keeps me very active - trying to hold down a 150 lb. rotweiller can be a chore! So my active job helps, but I have also been faithfully working out at the gym for about 5 years. When I don't go to the gym, I work out and run at my home. As for my complexion, I simply use facial scrubs and lots of moisturizer.

How do you feel in front of the camera?
At first I was very nervous. Although I've been in front of a camera many times, each time is different for me and for the first 30 minutes I am pretty nervous. After I get past that, I start to loosen up and begin to enjoy it.

What physical activities does your typical day consist of?
I do a lot of lifting, squatting, walking, and animal restraint at work. Working at a vet hospital really helps me to maintain a good physical appearance.

Do you consider yourself a healthy eater?
Yes, for the most part I am, but like any other normal female I get cravings. When I shop I try to get plenty of fresh vegetables and fruits and stay away from the fried foods.

What is your favorite food to binge on?
I don't really have a favorite. When I do get a craving, it varies from one food to another.

What do you hope to achieve by modelling?
Since I model only on a part-time basis, I haven't established unrealistic goals. I would of course love to become a supermodel, but realizing the unlikely chances of that happening I prefer to stay concentrated on the many other life opportunities.

Who is your role model (doesn't have to be a model)
This might sound odd but I have to say my fiance. I doubt that you really want to hear all the reasons why so I will save everyone the time, and just say that I admire and look up to him.

Which supermodel do you look up to the most?
No one in particular. I feel as though they all have their own special features and talents in the business. I admire them all for their hard work and determination. This business can be cruel and very difficult - anyone who succeeds has my utmost respect.

Would you ever pose in the nude?
Possibly but only if I felt it would benefit me in some way, so suffice it to say...probably not.

What, if anything, could persuade you to pose nude?
If after thoroughly considering the consequences, I decided that the job might enhance my modeling career rather than tarnish it, I would model nude. The opportunities for this kind of modeling are so very few, however, that I doubt I'll ever be "persuaded."

What would your family think of this choice?
I am the strong one in the family. I have always been told from my family that I had a good head on my shoulders. So, I feel that they would be supportive of my choices, as would my fiance.

What is the best beauty secret you know of that you utilize?
Before bed I wash my face with warm water and moisturize around my eyes. When I get up my skin feels like silk and no bags around the eyes.

Are you willing to travel?
Yes, I have already had to travel for jobs. I think travelling is my favorite part of the industry.

Are you willing to possibly relocate?
This again would have to be something that would significantly benefit me.

If you had your choice of where to live, where would you choose?
I would love to go back to TX. I love the land, people, and culture, but most of all the warm weather.
