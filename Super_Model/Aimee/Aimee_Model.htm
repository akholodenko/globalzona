Aimee's Interview

Name
Aimee King

Age
23

Marital status
married

Hometown
San Francisco,Ca

Education-where/when/level
I went to school in Mendocino, Ca until I was 12. Then I moved to Australia and went to a couple schools, Mt. Carmel Boarding School for a year then went to Canberra High School until I graduated in 1992.

Present job
Professional Model

Goals for future
To be happy, healthy and fullfilled in every goal I set out to achieve.

What got you interested in modelling?
I swear I was born like this, In my kindergarden photo I was already posing. Everyone told me I could never model and It pushed me even more into proving everyone was wrong!

How old were you?
I was 7 when I did my first runway show. I heard the department store was having a show, so I went to their office and refused to leave till they put me in their show.

What experience, if any, do you have modelling?
I have been modeling professionally for 7 years now and have worked for many designers including Yves Saint Laurent, Giorgio Armani, Ralph Lauren, Vivanne Westwood, etc.

What kind of modelling do you see yourself doing?
I see myself on the runway in Paris and Milan doing the collections/couture shows and editorial shoots for mags....

Is there anything about the modelling profession that scares you?
Bogus agencys and all those slimeballs!!!

What is your strongest feature?
I have to say my nose..everyone always said I need a nose job...I wouldn't do it and now it has worked to my advandage!

How do you take care of your body? complexion?
I drink alot of water and try not to wear make-up when I am not working. As far as my body I don't really have an exercise routine...I do go dancing as much as possiable (fun and good for you!!) and when I get the chance I go swimming....

How do you feel in front of the camera?
Like that is where I was born to be!!! I can sometimes be very shy but when I get behind the camera or on stage I transform, I feel at home.

What physical activities does your typical day consist of?
Every morning starts of with my mocha(or I am evil!) Then usually run around town going to castings with sometimes 200 other models and then throw in a fashion show or photo shoot . ..but In this business there Is no typical day!

Do you consider yourself a healthy eater?
Haha, well not by todays standards..I do eat anything I want, but the key is moderation..

What is your favorite food to binge on?
My god, there are so many...I would have to say double fudge chocolate cake (with ice cream of course!)

What do you hope to achieve by modelling?
To be on the cover of Vogue.. (and thats only one....)

Who is your role model (doesn't have to be a model)
Mmmm...let me think...I guess I would have to say my mom, because she is so smart, beautiful and won't take shit from anyone. Can't help but love that!!!

Which supermodel do you look up to the most?
I don't think I have a favorite..I like them all for different things. Linda for her chameleon like ability, Naomi for her runway walk and Giselle because she is just stunning...and thats only a few!

Would you ever pose in the nude?
Yes, and I have..I was not raised to be ashamed or embarrassed about being naked. I think If It's done tastefully and you are comfortable, there is nothing to be ashamed about..besides the body is a beautiful thing!!

What, if anything, could persuade you to pose nude?
I think that I covered this one above!!

What would your family think of this choice?
Well, my mom said if any of my nude photos were published that she would paste them to the side of the truck and drive around town!, and as far as my father goes, he has always supported me in my decisions.

What is the best beauty secret you know of that you utilize?
What makes you think Im going to spill my beauty secrets? After all they are secrets!! But I will give you one...for super smooth skin I smother myself in Palmers cocoa butter after my shower, It keeps you super smooth and it makes it even better that it smells like candy and my man loves it.

Are you willing to travel?
Is this a joke? of course I am willing to travel, It's one of the main perks from this job!!!

Are you willing to possibly relocate?
It's been known to happen...I lived in Australia for 5 years

If you had your choice of where to live, where would you choose?
If it was all up to me, I would have a home in about 5 citys all over the world and move around as I please.
