Interview
Name
Nic Skovera

Age
21

Marital status
Single

Hometown
Originally from Berkshires, but I have lived in Europe, Sarasota FL, Miami FL, Vermont , and currently in NYC.

Education-where/when/level
High School was great!! I loved it and had a blast. I took AP (Advanced Placement). In high school, I was on the soccer team, track team, cross country team & secretary of the wrestling team.

Present job
Full time, active NYC model. Actually I work in many places, and have done lots of work in Europe, as well as TV and movie work.

Goals for future
I have had many unique experiences and opportunities to see a lot of the world as a result of my modeling career. I feel a realistic goal of mine is to return to school in a few years and study psychology.

What got you interested in modelling?
When I was about 12 years old, my oldest sister's boyfrend was a photographer who kept telling me I should model. So finally one day we decided to give it a shot and we started doing fashion suppliments together. To be honest, I thought the idea of standing in front of a camera for a bunch of pictures was a waste of time because I was such a tomboy when I was growing up.

How old were you?
12

What experience, if any, do you have modelling?
When I was 16 yrs old, during the summer, I went to NY for Elite modeling agency, and started shooting with their photographers. Then I went to Europe and started doing fashion shows, commercials, editorial, calenders and covers. Now I've been in four calendars, continue doing lots of editorial work and fashion shows. Additionally, I've been in two films.

What kind of modeling do you see yourself doing?
I am actively doing: editorial work, fashion shows, calendars and covers. I've been told I am a very versatile model in front of the camera. Acting is also something that comes naturally to me. It's fun to be someone else once in a while.

Is there anything about the modelling profession that scares you?
I would say that it is disheartening when you hear stories about girls getting treated poorly. It is a business where you can set standards for yourself and still get work. I would encourage all aspiring models to set some standards for themselves, then you will strengthen your character when the time comes to put them into practice.

What is your strongest feature?
My strongest feature is who I am on the inside. You wouldn't know it to look at me because all models have to battle that preconcieved stereotype of being shallow. I'm a very deep, good, caring, and honest person. Others have said that my best feature is my face, lips, smile, long neck and hair. Makeup arttists always chime about my smooth flawless skin. But I'm happiest about my long legs, 47 1/2 inches. In the Guiness book of world records, Naja Armen is a supermodel with the longest legs, 47 inches, so I have her beat by 1/2 an inch!

How do you take care of your body? complexion?
I believe you need to take care of yourself inside and out, and maintain a healthy balance of everything. One little beauty secret my grandmother handed down to me, was to use witch hazel on my face. It works great, and gives me a clear complexion.

How do you feel in front of the camera?
Free. Like I can do anything or achieve anything. It is strange, because that is where people are happiest with me the most.

What physical activities does your typical day consist of?
Rushing, rushing, rushing, and more rushing around to jobs and castings all day long. That gives me a workout, but I do go to the gym to work all the muscle groups.

Do you consider yourself a healthy eater?
I eat mostly anything I want, and I eat only the best quality ice cream, but I try to make a point to eat healthy foods. Thank God for genetics!

What is your favorite food to binge on?
Pickles and ice cream. My mom always says, "when you get pregnant, you will finally eat normally."

What do you hope to achieve by modeling?
I think the 'adventurer' in me gave me the spirit to model, and the 'achiever' in me keeps me going to do my best on each project. It really is a mini-career that needs to be a positive time in your life, because when you are young and constantly meeting new people, you are developing social skills that will dictate how successful you will be after your modeling career.

Who is your role model (doesn't have to be a model)
God, and people who are kind, deep, good, and honest.

Which supermodel do you look up to the most?
Unfortunately I've seen so many things and heard so many stories, that I choose to be my own person with my my own goals and morals and do not mimic my life after any supermodel.

Would you ever pose in the nude?
Maybe, if it was by a well known photographer who had a reputation for excellent artistic work.

What, if anything, could persuade you to pose nude?
The point above, about it being done by a great photographer. Also, the reason behind the shoot. Honestly, I don´t see the point in being completely nude. I think it is more sensual when you leave something for the imagination. On the other hand, Emma Sjoberg and several other supermodels have posed nude and have had great careers.

What would your family think of this choice?
They always support me.

What is the best beauty secret you know of that you utilize?
The vibrant happy energy that comes from within. A positive attitude is good for the mind, body, and soul.

Are you willing to travel?
Yes. Travelling is fun and always a great learning experience.

Are you willing to possibly relocate?
Yes.

If you had your choice of where to live, where would you choose?
A deserted island with soft-white powdered sand, and blue-green glistening warm water. Not to mention orchards of sweet fruit at its peak of ripeness dangling from the trees. Twinkles from the stars and my arms wrapped around my soulmate. Ooops, got carried away :)
