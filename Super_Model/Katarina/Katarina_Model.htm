Katarina

Interview
Name
Katarina Pamucar

Age
20

Marital status
Single

Hometown
Rijeka, Croatia

Education-where/when/level
I'm a student at the Purdue Calumet University, which is located in suburbs of Chicago. Right now I'm a sophomore.

Present job
I work part time as an accountant, and I do modeling jobs around Chicago area on the side.

Goals for future
My dream is to finish school and major in International Relations. I want to travel, and visit every country in the world. I want to help minority groups all around the world, especially poor children in Eastern Europe, Africa, Asia, and Middle East. I also want to get married and have a nice family life, and be a mom one day, but I see that happening later on in my life. I would also like have a great modeling career ,and do runway shows, and print work for famous designers.last thing I want to do is visit all my relatives and friends back in Europe, which I have not seen in a few years because of a war.

What got you interested in modelling?
Many of my relatives and friends always told me to pursue a modeling career. They always used to tell me that I have the height and features. Most of all were the magazines. I always wanted to dress up and look glamorous and attract all the boys in my school.

How old were you?
I was only 13 years old

What experience, if any, do you have modelling?
I went to the modeling school in my old city for a few months. From there I got accepted in an agency. I was only 14 at the time. Later on I went a few times to Milan to do fashion shows. I also did some some modeling jobs around the area. Recently I began to do medeling here in Chicago. I did a few photo shoots, and I'm starting to travel around USA for modeling assignments.

What kind of modelling do you see yourself doing?
I see myself doing fashion shows (creating different attitudes while on runway), photo shoots for female/male magazines and, lots of glamour modeling where I can dress up in nice fancy outfits.

Is there anything about the modelling profession that scares you?
Yes of course there is. I'm scared that I will meet a client that will want to go farther than just doing business with me. I'm also scared of having someone that you trust and give them your whole career in hands and they destroy it, or betray you. I'm also scared that if I make it as a model, one day I will be out of work because my look will be faded.

What is your strongest feature?
I would have to say 2 things my eyes and my long legs. I have big deep eyes and I can make many different expressions. Also my legs because I always get compliments on them.

How do you take care of your body? complexion?
I exercise very often. I take lots of aerobics classes in my gym. I love to relax in sauna, whirl pool, and steam room. I love to feed my skin with nice extra silky protein lotions. For my completion I do not do much. Once in a while I put a cucumber mask on. I use a day and night cream to make it nice and soft. I also always make sure that I do not wear make up very often, and if I do I make sure before I go to sleep I took it all off.

How do you feel in front of the camera?
I love camera. I used to be very shy, but now I love it. I can take pictures for hours. It just seems right for me to work with a photographer in fron of it. I absolutely love shoots. I can change my personality, and play with it.

What physical activities does your typical day consist of?
As I said I go ti gym every day and I take lots of classes. I also do cardio workouts. I try to park my car far away where ever I park it, so that I can just walk more. I clean my house all the time. On weekend I like to dance. Sometimes I go for roller blading.

Do you consider yourself a healthy eater?
Yes I do . I became vegetarian 2 years ago and since them I have been eating healthy vegetables, fruits, proteins(and a little bit of sweets on the side).

What is your favorite food to binge on?
Chocolate. Wow I love it. Especially the European kind!

What do you hope to achieve by modelling?
I hope to be a successfull model. It was my dream to walk on the catwalk with super models. Also just prove to myself that I can do it, and that my dreams payed off. I would also do positive things with all the money I would get. I would send some back to my poor relatives in Croatia, and Serbia, and donate some money to needy people and organizations.

Who is your role model (doesn't have to be a model)
I adore princess Diana. She was my favorite lady. She was pretty, charming, but at the same time she was a very successful business lady, and a woman that helped people around the world.

Which supermodel do you look up to the most?
I have a few but my favorite of all would have to be Naomi. She is just gorgeous. I love her long legs, and hair. She just presents the woman that I want to be like (vert glamorous).

Would you ever pose in the nude?
I don't think that I would. I have a family that is very religious and I would never want to disappoint them, or embarrass my father.

What, if anything, could persuade you to pose nude?
As I said I would never do it, but maybe only for an exception I would if there was a good cause behind it. Like a few years ago when models posed nude for animal rights.

What would your family think of this choice?
They would not like it at all. They would be very mad at me for a long time.

What is the best beauty secret you know of that you utilize?
I like to put wet chamomile bags under my eyes and after a few minutes when I take them off my eyes look very fresh, and there is no circles or reddish areas around my eyes.

Are you willing to travel?
Yes it is a big part of modeling world and I would not mind it at all. If fact I love to travel, adn this would give me opportunity to see a lot of different parts of the world.

Are you willing to possibly relocate?
Yes I am. I do know that usually models need to move to a big city to pursue theit carrer. That is just a part of the business, and it makes you a good model if you do travel and relocate.

If you had your choice of where to live, where would you choose?
It would be in Rio De Janeiro. Need to say more? It has great beaches, people are always happy and in a good mood, and it is the prettiest city that I ever saw.
