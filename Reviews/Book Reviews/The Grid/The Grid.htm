THE GRID

Synopsis
Welcome to a modern - day sci - fi story about an architectural firm, which is ending the work on a new building dubbed the "Gridiron". The building is "top of the line" hi - tech and luxurious, with the computer, Abraham, controlling all of the systems, including life - support, security, restrooms, and nearly everything else. Everything is going smoothly - until, suddenly, dead bodies of the employees begin to show up through out the building and at the climax, the employees get trapped, with no way out but death.

Review
Books are written to make the reader think and that is exactly the characteristic The Grid achieves. As mentioned in the synopsis, the novel is set on the site of a newly constucted smart building in which a computer system goes haywire and begins to kill the occupants.

This idea stretches the ideas of today, since we are living in and dealing with a computer age - the I-Generation. The mere thought of computers taking over has been implemented in a number of written, as well as filmed works. Movies that erected the words of an artificially - minded self - learning machine include the Terminator series, the Universal Soldier series and many others.

While the plot of the book does not seem to have an completely original soul, the author gives it its own life, with realistic characters and conversations. The novel is great, easy to read, and goes by in a flash. The Grid is on our recommended list.
Book Info.
Category: Action Thriller
Written by: Philip Kerr
Rating: B+
