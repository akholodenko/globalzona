OCEAN'S 11

Synopsis
A remake of the classic Rat Pack caper, the latest film from Oscar-winning director Steven Soderbergh (Traffic) boasts an all-star cast: George Clooney, Julia Roberts, Matt Damon, Brad Pitt, and Don Cheadle, among others. The movie follows a group of crooks, led by Danny Ocean (Clooney), as they plan a major heist of a Vegas casino.


Review
       When choosing the ultimate basketball team, names such as Shaquille O'Neal, Kobe Bryant, and Michael Jordan come up. When choosing the ultimate movie cast, names such as George Clooney, Brad Pitt, and Julia Roberts come up. "Ocean's 11" comes off as an all-star movie with an all-star cast.
       Every character plays their role to the fullest, bringing power and excitement to the screen. Clooney plays the leader of an operation and plays the role with the charm it deserves. Pitt adds to thje film with his role as the right hand of Clooney's character. Others fall in appropriate positions, with each persona filling unique shoes. However, it seemed that the part played by Julia Roberts as the ex-wife of Clooney and now the love interest of his foe was not suited for her. The role needed a younger, less seriously dramatic actress, yet Roberts pushed the role for all it had to give.
       The plot shows deep thought and originality on the part of the writters, who came up with more twists than ever seen before. This film is a must-see and should be at the top of every movie-goes list.
Movie Info.
Category: Action Comedy
Starring:George Clooney, Julia Roberts, Brad Pitt, Matt Damon
Directed by: Steven Soderbergh
Rated: PG-13
Running Time : 114
Rating: A
