BAD BOYS II

Synopsis
Will Smith and Martin Lawrence return as buddy-detectives Mike Lowrey and Marcus Burnett on a case that leads them to a giant criminal conspiracy in London. Meanwhile, Lowrey’s feelings for Burnett’s younger sister (Gabrielle Union) make their jobs more complicated.



Review
       What can you say about Will and Martin in a movie together. They proved themselves as worthy compliments in the first Bay Boys film and they do it again in the sequel.
       With nonstop action and adventure, the film gives the audience the ride of their lives that is worth every buck. The story develops well, tying various humor that expands throughout the movie and keeps you laughing.
       Although the story is complete the film is a bit longer than desired. While most action movies are cut at about 90-100 minutes, Bad Boys 2 goes on for a whopping 146 minutes. The action is nonstop, but could have been a bit shorter. Overall this is a must see film for all action fanatics.
Movie Info.
Category: Action
Starring:Will Smith, Martin Lawrence, Jordi Molla, Gabrielle Union
Directed by: Michael Bay
Rated: R
Running Time : 146
Rating: B+
