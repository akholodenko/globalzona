BLESS THE CHILD

Synopsis
Based on the supernatural thriller by Cathy Cash Spellman, Kim Basinger stars as a psychiatric nurse who begins to suspect that her six-year-old niece is in danger from members of a Satanic sect who plan to use her in service of a Biblical prophecy. Jimmy Smits plays a police detective also out to foil the cult.
