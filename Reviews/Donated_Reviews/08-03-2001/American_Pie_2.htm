AMERICAN PIE 2

Synopsis
year after the wildly popular first film, Heather (Mena Suvari), is off studying in Europe, which means that her devoted boyfriend, Oz (Chris Klein), is left back home. And he's 19 years old, which means that he's forced to struggle with...temptation! Jason Biggs is back, too, but no word as to whether he'll fornicate with a pie in this one.

Review
        They are back for a second time around - the extremely popular everyday characters of the original and still unbeatable "American Pie". While a year older in body, the guys don't seem to be a year older in mind: one still wants Nadia, another Stifler's mom, third Vicky, and last just wants random reckless action.
        While trying to match the entertainment value and originality of the first, "American Pie 2" misses by a short margin. The characters were there, the story was there, the setting was there, but something wasn't right. In the original we were initially introduced to people every teenager can easily relate to. However, the second installment brought about the same characters (which are not described or introduced again whatsoever and it is simply required for the audience to have seen the first in order to understand the second), yet now in less believable situations.
        Perhaps it seems that the target audience of the first feature has grown during the past two years, while the characters have only grown a year and matured none. All in all, the film is fun to watch and you really should go see it. However, don't expect to come out of the theater with the same feeling as the original. Of course this does not apply to everyone, so depend on your own personal opinion and judgment.
Movie Info.
Category: Comedy
Starring:Jason Biggs, Shannon Elizabeth, Alyson Hannigan, Chris Klein
Directed by: J.B. Rogers
Rated: R
Running Time : 104
Rating: B+
