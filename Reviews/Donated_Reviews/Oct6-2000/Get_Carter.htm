GET CARTER

Synopsis
Sylvester Stalone stars in this remake of the 1971 British thriller. Obsessed with finding his brother's killer, Jack Carter scours Seattle, investigating his sister-in-law and questioning his brother's friends, turning up in the process a cyber-sex ring and some gangsters who would prefer that Carter leave things be.

Review
     "Pay Back"...oh, I'm sorry, "Get Carter", is a film of one man beating all of the odds and coming out on top. While hopefully not Stallone's last film, "Get Carter" is a type of "Pay Back" and "Get Shorty" type of a movie.
     After coming back to a world he left behind, Jack Carter [Stallone] tries to uncover what seems to him as a set up for his brother's murder. Meeting new and running into old friends, Jack runs in circles between five other characters [brother's business partner, brother's lady of the night, cyber sex ring leader, and a Bill Gates type of a billionaire]. During this time, his boss from Las Vegas keeps bugging him about Jack's love interest who is also the boss's wife/girlfriend.
     While an overall interesting story that leads to a surprising, yet not shocking conclusion, "Get Carter" does have the audience looking at their watch during some or other point of the film. It took the film a good 60% of its running time to heat up to a point of complete interest. However, if you are a big Stallone fan, you will totally enjoy the production and the image the character of Jack Carter conveys to the audience.
Movie Info.
Category: Action Thriller
Starring: Sylvester Stallone, Michael Caine and Rachael Leigh Cook
Directed by: Stephen T Kay
Rated: R
Running time: 147 min.
Rating: C+
