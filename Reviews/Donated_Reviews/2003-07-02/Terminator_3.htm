TERMINATOR 3: RISE OF THE MACHINES

Synopsis
John Connor (Nick Stahl) is hunted down by a female Terminator, the T-X, sent from the future with the power to control all machines around her in this third installment of the sci-fi/action saga. Arnold Schwarzenegger returns as a cyborg sent back in time to protect him.




Review
       The lightning strikes, the ground melts, the smoke clears, and a machine covered by skin fibers appears. We all know this scene very well from the blockbuster hits The Terminator and Terminator 2: Judgment Day. However, when news of a third film being in the works surfaces doubt comes about as well. Lucky, T3 goes on to hold up the Terminator story line well, adding new twists and realizations to the plot.
       As it slowly makes it's audience see that fact that destiny is inevitable and while able to delay events, humanity cannot avoid them, the story of T3 takes people on a wild action ride of car chases, cyborg fights, and of course lots of Arnold.
       One of the aspects of the film that took a bit of getting use to was the fact that John Connor was played by a new actor. In situations like this adjustments have to be made since every actor bring his own edits to the character. However, by the end of the film he had perfectly filled the shoes of the savior of the world. Another negative aspect was the fact that the film in less than two hours long. While a plus for those annoyed with three hour epics, in comparison to the nearly two and a half hour long T2, the film did not have enough runaway to take off as a full epic adventure.
       The conclusion is quiet unexpected and bring the entire story full circle. Generally this film is a must see for Terminator fans, and shouldn't be missed by other as well.
Movie Info.
Category: Action Sci-Fi
Starring:Arnold Schwarzenegger, Nick Stahl, Claire Danes, Kristanna Loken
Directed by: Jonathan Mostow
Rated: R
Running Time : 110
Rating: B
