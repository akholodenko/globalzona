WHAT'S THE WORST THAT
COULD HAPPEN?

Synopsis
A billionaire (Danny DeVito) catches a thief (Martin Lawrence) robbing his mansion and, to get even, he takes his lucky ring ("I robbed a thief! How could you not see the humor in that?"). Deprived of his good-luck charm and faced with a future of bad karma, the thief begins plotting revenge on the haughty tycoon.

Review
       When two humor powerhouses combine for a comedy that combines laughs from old and new schools of Hollywood that only thing possible to come out is aching sides.
       In the film, Danny DeVito puts on an excellent image of a heartless billionaire, making the lighthearted comedy even more interesting to watch. As he tries to stay one step ahead of the character played by Martin Lawrence, comical and satirical situations come about.
       Humoring on the relationship between the head of security and the rich man also brings punchlines to the screen. Other characters make the film irresistible and a must see!
Movie Info.
Category: Action Comedy
Starring:Martin Lawrence, Danny DeVito, John Leguizamo, Nora Dunn
Directed by: Sam Weisman
Rated: PG-13
 Rating: B
