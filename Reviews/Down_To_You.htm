Down To You
by
Artem Kholodenko
The first thing that I can tell you related to this movie is not to listen to critics and see it for your self! Although insulted by various informative sources, the film is an excellent display of real-life relationships. The basic plot of the film is about a couple that goes through the basic stages of their relationship. The plot - although hardly original - is entertaining and realistic.
While watching the film I recollected some on my own personal memories of my relationship and tried to overlay it with the film. The basic characteristics were there except for one major factor - my is still going strong! Anyway, back to the issue at hand. The characters in the film create an interesting atmosphere, especially the minor roles or a nipple-piercing muscle man and a porn-star director. While irrelevant to the plot, the two produce a humorous environment that appeals to the viewer.
Some of the criticism for the film came form it's time-skipping plot, which in actuality is perfectly normal. Although it may seem like most of the important events occur close to the finale that is untrue. All scenes are important, no matter how miniscule. The small things of the relationship are displayed in those moments and if you don't catch that, then you miss a big chunk of the picture.
"Down To You" is nowhere close to an Oscar contender, but it satisfies the questionable mind of teenagers and other alike. To some, love and relationship questions are answered with this modern-day Cinderella story, to others, information confirmed. Whatever you might be in search of, don't listen to others, just go and see the movie. We gave it a B+.
