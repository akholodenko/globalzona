3OHA ADK Rating: A-
Rating Legend
-
Called the scariest sci-fi film of the year, "Pitch Black" does fit the shoes. With an unpredictable plot, the movie keeps your toe on edge and your pants wet.
The plot involves a group of crash-landers on a strange, three-sunned planet. Besides the problem of a killer convict, the individuals face deadly alien creatures. Everything is fine, as along as you stay in daylight - no problem on a planet where there are three suns. Only one problem: every twenty-two years a lasting eclipse takes place and evil comes to the surface - are you afraid of the dark?
When I came out of the theater I had a mixed felling about the movie: it was at the same time bad and good. In the end the positive aspects of the film, such as the main character and specials effects took over the negative features of the story line. This film probably appeals more to the sci-fi lovers, but does have grasping aspects, which will hook average moviegoers as well.
Some of the surprises in the movie have to do with a secret, a death, and a special gift. Overall, now you should have enough information about "Pitch Black" to whether be psyched or disgusted by it. One thing to remember is that all reviews are made from the critic's perspective and your ideas might not match the writers'. So decide everything for yourself and don't blame me if you are a good movie or throw away five bucks.
