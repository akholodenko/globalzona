[10.10.00.AD]
        I had an interesting weekend this time around. On all of the days for something to go wrong, I got a chipped tooth Friday night. So I stayed at home evening. On Saturday I was suppose to go and visit some college friends, yet my ride there was leaving in the afternoon, while my emergency appointment was at 5pm. That totally killed the mood. So I went in at 5pm to get my tooth fixed up.
        Surprisingly, when I got to the dentist's office I didn't have to wait to be taken care of and it took less than 40 minutes. I got there, they took x-rays with fear that I might need the dreaded root canal. That is the way it looked on the slide, however, when the doctor started to drill [this of course after numerous shot sin the cheek to numb it] he didn't find anything. So instead of the root canal and a crown, I was simply patched up with some cement and let go off. That was the best dentist appointment I have ever had.
[10.05.00.AD]
        A new month has come to us. Once again time is flying at rates of unimaginable proportions. No one knows where it all might lead. .
        Let's see, what have I been up to. Well, I went to get some ice cream during the weekend with some friends. It was cool, but sucks that the night had to be cut short. I guess I am getting too use to coming home at 2 or 3 in the morning. I have to cut down on that in general, because I am killing my body by going to sleep late and awaking early in the morning.
        School is really killing me. It is not the problem of homework or anything, it is more about the stress of everyday life in school. I never thought that I would be hit this hard mentally. I guess that while last year was tough on homework and all, this year is going to take the living soul out of me. I hope that I can survive, and I will, with the help of the people around me.
